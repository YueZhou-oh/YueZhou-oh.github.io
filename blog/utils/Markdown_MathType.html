Markdown MathTyping

Two ways of add a new equation in markdown:

  • Inline equation with $ ... $: Γ(z)=xxx
  • Independent equation with $$ ... $$:
Γ(z)=xxx

  • $\lbrace a+b\rbrace$: {a+b}
  • $\langle x \rangle$: ⟨x⟩
  • $\lceil x \rceil$: ⌈x⌉
  • $\lfloor x \rfloor$: ⌊x⌋
